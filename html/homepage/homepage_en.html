Welcome to Reperio

The USI search portal inside swisscovery

Here you can perform simple and advanced searches for books, e-books, journals, databases and other media.

Through Reperio you'll benefit from:

  • Access to the collections of over 470 scientific libraries in Switzerland
  • Use of electronic resources licenced by your library or Open Access
  • Document requests to libraries, directly or via SLSP-Courier
  • Advanced management of our account, your loans, your requests

Registration

To access Reperio's features and library services you need to register.

Reperio is organized in several research scopes.
